# Addy's Parrot

**Replay every sentence until you nail it.**

Addy's Parrot is a free desktop app for practising English with any YouTube video — or any video file on your computer. Paste a link, and it loops one sentence at a time, shows you the dictionary for any word, records your voice so you can hear yourself, and brings back the sentences you found hard tomorrow.

Everything runs **on your own computer**. No account, no ads, no tracking.

## What it does

- **Any YouTube video, or a file from your computer** — paste a link or drag a file in. Files never leave your machine.
- **Subtitles generated locally** — transcription runs entirely on your computer, no upload.
- **Tap a sentence to loop it** — it repeats until you stop. Drop to 0.5× / 0.75× when you need to.
- **Record yourself and listen back** — hear how your shadowing actually sounded.
- **Click any word for the dictionary** — pronunciation, meaning, and examples translated into your native language.
- **Save the words and lines you want to keep** — everything stored in one file on your computer.
- **Spaced repetition** — saved items come back on an FSRS schedule: hard ones tomorrow, easy ones next week.
- **13 interface languages** — English, Simplified & Traditional Chinese, Spanish, Hindi, Arabic, Portuguese, French, Japanese, German, Korean, Thai, Indonesian.

## Privacy

Addy's Parrot processes your videos and your voice entirely on your device. There is no server and no account. The app only touches the network to download a small set of tools on first launch, check for updates (which you can turn off), fetch YouTube videos when you paste a link, and look up dictionary definitions.

## System requirements

- **Mac** — macOS 12 (Monterey) or later, Apple Silicon or Intel. ~600 MB free.
- **Windows** — Windows 10 (build 1809) or Windows 11, 64-bit. ~800 MB free.
